Luxury maternity Event Management Services

A maternity ceremony is one of the most colorful and joyful wedding celebrations. It brings together family, friends, music, dance and traditions before the wedding day.

At Ayojan House, we specialize in creating premium maternity experiences with floral decor, custom seating, entertainment zones, artist coordination, catering and complete event management solutions.

Whether you prefer a traditional marigold theme, a boho floral setup, a royal palace theme or a modern luxury design, our team ensures every detail reflects your vision.

Our maternity event planning services are available throughout Patna, Danapur, Bihta, Hajipur and other parts of Bihar.

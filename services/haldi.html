About Haldi Ceremony Planning

Beautiful Haldi Celebrations Designed Around You

The Haldi ceremony is one of the most cherished pre-wedding rituals in Indian weddings. Families gather together to bless the bride and groom with turmeric paste, symbolizing purity, prosperity and happiness. At Ayojan House, we design luxurious Haldi celebrations featuring floral backdrops, traditional decor elements, customized seating arrangements and seamless event management throughout Patna and Bihar.

Whether your guest list is 50 people or 1000 people, we design every Haldi event uniquely around your style, budget and vision.

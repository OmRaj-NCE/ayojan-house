Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

✦

How early should I book Ayojan House for my event?+

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance for weddings and major events, and 4–8 weeks for birthdays and smaller functions. Peak season (November–February) fills up quickly in Patna, so earlier bookings guarantee your preferred dates.

Do you manage events outside Patna?+

Absolutely! While our home base is Danapur, Patna, we manage events across all major cities and towns in Bihar — including Gaya, Muzaffarpur, Bhagalpur, Nalanda and beyond. Outstation projects are handled with the same quality and dedication.

What is the minimum budget for a wedding package?+

We offer flexible packages starting from ₹50,000 for simple events and going up based on scale, décor complexity, catering, and additional services. We believe every family deserves a beautiful event, so we always work within your budget.

Do you provide catering services too?+

Yes! We offer in-house catering with authentic Bihari, North Indian, Mughlai, Continental and Chinese cuisines. Our kitchen team serves everything from traditional litti-chokha to five-course continental dinners for corporate galas.

Can I see your past work before booking?+

Of course! Visit our Gallery page to browse photos and videos from 50+ real events. You can also visit our office at Mithila Colony, Danapur, Patna to view physical portfolios and speak directly with our design team.

What happens if there is a last-minute change in the event?+

Life is unpredictable — and we are ready for it! Our dedicated event managers are on-call 24/7 during event days. We have contingency plans for weather, vendor delays, and on-day changes. Our team adapts instantly so your event goes perfectly regardless.

How do I get started with Ayojan House?+

Simply call us at +91 82108 56330, fill our online contact form, or walk into our office. We start with a free consultation call to understand your vision, event date, guest count and budget — and then we build your dream from there!
